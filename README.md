#REASONS WHY SSH IS SAFER THAN HTTPS PASSWORDS
_1._ SSH keys have massive cryptograhic lengths which unlike HTTPS passwords are impossible for a third-party to guess.

_2._ SSH keys do not feature typing. This way hackers cannot steal it by watching your screen or tracking your keystrokes.

_3._ There's no sharing with SSH keys. Passwords are not sent to the server for authentication like it happens with HTTPS passwords.

_4._ Digital signatures are sent over the internet. They are not typed like passwords.

_5._ SSH keys cannot be reused on other sites like HTTPS passwords. This way, if a hacker is able to break into one of your accounts somehow, there's no way he or she can do same with your other accounts.
